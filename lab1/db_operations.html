6. Additional operations





On the bottom section, you can see Additional Operations that will be explored in later labs. Additional Operations explore the capabilities of drone usage in:

Operations Operation Descriptions
Rescue Coordination Assisting First Responders in evacuation routes, scouting in dangerous locations, and traffic surveillance
Stormwater Operations Monitoring Assisting First Responders in evacuation routes due to flooding, remotely checking water levels, and checking on equipment placement
Safety Analysis Scanning and analyzing areas to determine civilian safety
Damage Inspection Investigating buildings and homes for structural damage where it is risky for human inspection
Geothermal Monitoring Monitoring geothermal areas where humans cannot monitor due to heat and danger

These are just a handful of use cases that drone technology, edge computing and cognitive analysis are used for. These use cases are focused on the Public Market and can be expanded to many other industries.
