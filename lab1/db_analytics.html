5. Drone analytics





In the middle section, you can see multiple cards that display drone analytics and insights. These cards display vital information for the drone(s) selected. This information includes:

  • Previous Flight - A specific flight record can be chosen from dropdown list to see the flight details. The map on the left shows the corresponding flight route animation and the user can see how the drone flew intuitively.
  • Your Flight Status - Displays the average flight time and speed for all drones
  • Flight Operations - Shows the allocation of drones for specific operations and usages
  • Operations Statistics - Drills down into the Flight Operations statistics and show the exact tasks of the drones in each Operation
  • Analytics Usage - Displays the usage of a drone and comparison in usage over time
