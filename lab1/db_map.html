2. Map view





In order to operate the drone, an understanding of the environment is crucial for safe and effective operation. The map view card contains layers that contain a large amount of data that is displayed within the Remote Operations Console.

You can toggle on and off the drone layer in order to hide/show the positions of the drones on the map.

In this lab and future labs, you will interact with these layers on the map:

  • Drone Layer - Display available drones in chosen area
  • Weather Layer - See real-time weather overlays from Weather Company data
  • Landmarks and Points of Interest Layer - Highlight key locations near drones such as police/firefighter presence and the IBM Global Solution Center in Coppell, TX
  • Incidents Layer - Display real-time incidents in the city
